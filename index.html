How we do it

Our focus is help you and youor teeam understand how you all work, and use your stregths to truly collaborate. We look for ways to pull out the best for each of you within the personal boundries that keep each person comfortable. If this means zoom calls with cameras off, using notion to make tasks list or casual coffee meetups to just talk, we will find what works best within your team and help you create more.

We want to make sure that all team members don't feel the urge to proclaim, "What a drag!". So we focus on shacking up the work enviorment to have a variety of collaborative sapces.

We understand team need to happen, and that sometime they have to be more traditional but having interesting ways to implement traditional ways keeps things fresh.

We want to help you work through difference of visions by looking at things from a new perspective. Can we use both ideas? Are they that far off from each other? Is there something we are missing?

If you need a second or thrid set of ears/mind to spring board off of we are hear. We can schedule brainstorm sessions even if it's just for someone to prepare for thir big presentation.

We like to help the team remember y'all are kick ass and the day, creative block, or whatever obstable is ahead. So punch that day in the face it will feel good.

We are hear to cheer you on and remind you all progress is that progress. So take that small or big step. Do something great today!
